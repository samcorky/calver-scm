# Code of Conduct

We want this project to be welcoming, respectful, and safe for everyone.

## Expected behaviour

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent and ask for clarification before escalating.
- Give feedback on ideas, not people.
- Be patient with contributors of all experience levels.

## Unacceptable behaviour

- Harassment, threats, or abusive language.
- Discrimination or hateful conduct.
- Personal attacks, trolling, or deliberate disruption.
- Sharing someone’s private information without permission.

## Scope

This applies to project spaces (issues, pull requests, discussions, and other community channels) and to public spaces when representing this project.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behaviour, report it privately via:

https://github.com/samcorky/calver-scm/security/advisories/new

If you cannot use that channel, open a minimal public issue requesting a private contact method and do not include sensitive details.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove content, pause discussions, or restrict participation for conduct that violates this policy, based on severity and patterns of behaviour.
